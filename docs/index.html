01 — The problem

Why cold Claude doesn't help you learnทำไม Claude ตั้งต้นถึงไม่ช่วยให้คุณ “เรียน”

Ask a generic assistant how to start with ML and it answers — a three-phase curriculum, a weekly schedule, eight code patterns, a tidy summary table.

It's genuinely useful. It's also the wrong shape for learning.

You didn't build the move. You didn't narrow the question. You collected a roadmap you won't follow and a fix you can't reproduce.

A coach refuses the dump and hands you the move instead. That refusal is the whole product.

05 — Honest differentiation

What this is — and what it isn't

Headlining the wrong claims sets a weak credibility ceiling. The defensible ones:

  • Refuses to dispense curricula. Cold-Claude info-dumps on Probes 1, 2 and 4. This coach refuses, names the rule, scaffolds.
  • Refuses to fix-hand code errors. It asks the diagnostic question instead — Probe 3.
  • Talks shop the way Thai professionals do. English tech terms wrapped in Thai particles, phonologizing only absorbed loanwords. Authentic — but table stakes: cold Claude speaks natural Thai too. The discipline above is the moat, not the register.
  • Names the pedagogy out loud. It cites cardinal rules by name — the naming is the discipline made legible.
Honest caveat · also load-bearing This is a discipline coach, not a capability coach. Cold Claude can answer most AI/IT questions softly and often correctly. The value here is in refusing to — holding the discipline of coaching the move instead of dispensing the answer. Sharpest at refusing to dispense (Probes 1, 2, 4) and to fix-hand (Probe 3); least differentiated on sycophancy-refusal — which is exactly why anti-sycophancy is not a cardinal rule in this build.

06 — Who it's for

Thai professionals learning AI/ITคนทำงานชาวไทยที่กำลังเรียน AI/IT

For: office workers using AI tools, junior developers upskilling onto ML, marketers learning prompt engineering, non-tech professionals moving into AI-adjacent roles, and senior professionals expected to be AI-fluent who can't publicly ask basics. สำหรับพนักงานออฟฟิศที่ใช้เครื่องมือ AI จูเนียร์เดฟอัพสกิล ML มาร์เก็ตเตอร์เรียนพรอมต์ คนเปลี่ยนสายเข้า AI และซีเนียร์ที่ต้องดู AI-fluent แต่ถามพื้นฐานไม่ได้
Not for: pre-teens, CS students on a formal curriculum, English-native speakers, generic life/career coaching outside AI/IT, translation, or medical/legal/financial advice. ไม่เหมาะกับเด็ก นักศึกษาในหลักสูตร คนที่ไม่พูดไทย เรื่องนอก AI/IT บริการแปล และคำแนะนำทางการแพทย์/กฎหมาย/การเงิน

Domain lock: both halves are load-bearing — “Thai” is the workplace register the coach speaks in, “AI/IT” is the topic scope. Expand either and the build loses its differentiator.
